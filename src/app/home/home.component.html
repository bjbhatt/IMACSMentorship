Welcome To IMACS Mentorship Program

The Mentorship Program is designed to connect IMACS Members around the globe in order to provide opportunities to teach, learn, and collaborate. Below are two options to Become a Mentor or Get a Mentor with which IMACS Members can expand their role in the IMACS community. Please Note: To be eligible to be an IMACS Mentor, you must be at least 5 years out from your post doctorate or fellowship training program.
